Mash Petitions is a digital public service that enables citizens to explore information about UK government and parliament petitions. It builds upon the government e-petitions platform, exposes more information and combines that with other public open data.

It is a free service and intended for social good, to improve government transparency, enable effective lobbying and provide interesting analysis.
