Mash Petitions is a digital service to help citizens explore information about UK government parliamentary petitions. It uses the government e-petitions platform and builds upon it by exposing more information and combining that with other open public data sources.

It is free and intended for social good, to improve government transparency, enable effective campaigning and provide interesting analysis.
